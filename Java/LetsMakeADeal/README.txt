LetsMakeADeal.java is a program that simulates the game show "Lets Make A Deal". First, second, and third prizes are randomly assigned by way of a shuffled array list of strings. The contestants curtain choices are selected using the same method as the prize assignments. The contestant who chooses the worst curtain of the two is eliminated and the other remaining contestant has the option to switch their curtain or to keep it. After running the program 1,000 times, switching proved benefitial 531 times and failed 469 times. Refraining from switching worked 499 times and failed 501.
